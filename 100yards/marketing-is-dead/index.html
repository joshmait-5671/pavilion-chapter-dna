Case Study
A German company that refused to pivot.
01
They didn't customize for Americans.
SAP told the Fortune 500 their processes were broken. They forced the client to standardize to the software — not the other way around.
02
They built a priesthood, not a sales team.
Accenture, Deloitte, entire consulting practices built around implementing the German standard. The ecosystem sold for them.
03
They won the infrastructure, not the desktop.
Today, 99 of the 100 largest companies in the world run on SAP. They didn't win by being easy. They won by being inevitable.
Their "defiance" was their strategy. Yours can be too.
