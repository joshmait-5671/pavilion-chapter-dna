2026 ICHRA Roadmap — Executive Summary

Market State

HealthSherpa estimates the ICHRA market at 400,000–800,000 enrolled members as of January 1, 2026, growing roughly 3X year-over-year. With 28% growth year-to-date (through March 2026), there's a strong case the market has now crossed 1 million members. Notably, 82–83% of ICHRA enrollment is off-exchange, and ICHRA members are buying up on metal levels compared to on-exchange enrollees.

Risk Pool Insight

ICHRA members skew younger (64% under 45 vs. 58% for broader ACA). Carriers that can segment their off-exchange base report that ICHRA risk performs more like on-exchange than the broader off-exchange pool — a critical finding for carriers concerned about adverse selection.

2026 Product Roadmap

EnrollConnect (launching April 13): Full programmatic enrollment API. One canonical schema, every carrier. Platforms build once and get all carriers — no more carrier-by-carrier integrations. 67% reduction in application time proven via DeepLinks.

PayConnect (late 2026): ACH-only premium clearinghouse. HealthSherpa collects, aggregates, and remits to carriers in bulk. Eliminates manual binder entry and insulates carriers from credit card fees (up to 4%).

StatusConnect: Webhook-based policy lifecycle tracking. Effectuation, payment status, termination — daily carrier feeds delivered programmatically.

QuoteConnect: Quoting API available now. Shop by Doc coming summer 2026. Formulary search scoped for 2027. Goal: substantially all plans, all carriers before OE27.

Carrier Expansion

Current: 21 live off-exchange carrier integrations (~40% market footprint). Goal: 65 carriers by OE27 (~65–70% market coverage). Medical Mutual, Samford, Security, and CareSource of Wisconsin going live this week.

Key Positioning

"Our goal is to enable all of you, and compete with none." HealthSherpa is infrastructure — a many-to-one-to-many solution. 40+ administrator partners through a single integration, at no cost, and not AOR.

ICHRA Is Outperforming Carrier Expectations. Here's the Proof.

Data from HealthSherpa's horizontal view across 40+ ICHRA administrator platforms shows a market that's both healthier and growing faster than most carrier projections assumed.

Risk pool reality: Carriers that can segment their off-exchange base report that ICHRA risk performs more like on-exchange than the broader OFX pool. This directly counters the adverse selection concern that's kept many carriers on the sideline.

Metal level signal: ICHRA members are buying up — enrolling in higher silver levels off-exchange than on-exchange, driven by lower rate increases and better value propositions without silver loading.

The payment problem you already know about: Nearly 100% credit card adoption is costing carriers up to 4% in transaction fees on unsubsidized premiums. CMS won't allow convenience fees. HealthSherpa's PayConnect (late 2026) offers an ACH-only clearinghouse — bulk daily remittance with full reconciliation, insulating carriers from both credit card fees and ACH clawbacks.

The infrastructure question: HealthSherpa now provides a single integration point to 40+ ICHRA platforms. For carriers, that's one integration instead of 40 — and it scales as the market scales.
