How it works

Three steps. No awkwardness.

01

Tell us about you

Your function, your stage, how you like to work. Takes 2 minutes. We use this to find someone who actually makes sense — not a random match.

02

We find your match

Our algorithm pairs you with a peer based on function, company stage, timezone, and how you like to engage. Within a week of launch, you'll have your buddy.

03

Start the conversation

You get a warm intro. Take it from there. Slack each other, hop on a call, share what's working. We check in at 4 and 8 weeks to make sure it's great.

What we match on

This isn't random.

We match on what actually matters — not just job title. Every match is scored across five dimensions so your first conversation starts on solid ground.

  • 01
    Function — Same department, same language. A marketer matched with a marketer.
  • 02
    Company stage — Similar revenue, similar problems. A growth-stage operator matched with another.
  • 03
    Location & timezone — Same city if possible. Same timezone at minimum. Easy to connect.
  • 04
    Engagement style — Weekly calls? Async Slack? We match how you want to work, not just who you are.
  • 05
    AI experience — Peers, not mentors. Matched at the same level so nobody's teaching down.

Questions.

How long does a buddy pairing last?
As long as you want. We check in at 4 and 8 weeks to make sure it's working. After 8 weeks, you can keep your buddy, get a new match, or add a second one. Some pairs stay together for months. Your call.
Who are the buddies?
Other GTM operators — people in your function, at a similar company stage, figuring out AI at the same pace. This isn't mentorship. It's a peer who gets what you're dealing with.
Is this free?
Yes. The AI Buddy Program is included with Pavilion membership. Non-members can also join — it's a great way to experience the Pavilion community.
When does it launch?
As soon as we hit 100 signups. We want enough people in the pool to make great matches from day one. Early signups get priority matching.
