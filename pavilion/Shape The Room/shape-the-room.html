Why This Exists

The room is only as good as the people in it.

Pavilion doesn't grow through advertising. It grows through the judgment of the people already inside.

Every strong introduction raises the floor. Every weak one lowers it. We're asking you to take that seriously.

What We Ask

Once a month.
One person.
Your judgment.

Every month, members receive a single question: who do you know that belongs here?

Not a list. Not a blast to your LinkedIn network. One specific operator you've worked with, know well, and would genuinely vouch for. Someone you'd want in the room with you.

Submit their name. We handle the outreach and mention you by name. No awkward asks on your end.

This is not a program. It is a norm.

What Makes Someone Right

You know them when you think of them.

They are not someone you're doing a favor for. They are someone you'd genuinely vouch for without hesitation.

A strong introduction is:

  • Currently in a senior operator role — VP and above in B2B
  • Has real GTM responsibility: revenue, pipeline, or customer outcomes
  • In-seat, not between roles
  • Gives more than they take in any room they're in
  • Would make at least one other Pavilion member better for being here
How It Works

Simple by design.

Three steps. The whole thing takes two minutes.

Step 01

Think of one person.

Who is one operator you know right now who should be in this room? Not a list. One person.

Step 02

Submit their name.

Tell us who they are, how you know them, and one line on why they belong. That's the whole form.

Step 03

We follow up.

Pavilion reaches out directly and mentions your name. You don't have to do anything else.
