## Site Audit Findings

### Funnel Map
```
Homepage → /qualify → [3 buttons]
  "I'm an in-seat operator"   → /join  (3 steps: Basic info, Career info, Checkout)
  "I'm a fractional operator" → /access (4 steps: review/waitlist flow)
  "I'm an investor or CEO"    → /access (same form as fractional)
```

### Top 5 CRO Issues Identified

**1. /pricing is the real decision page**
- Gets more views than the homepage (1,902 vs 1,899)
- Prices hidden behind two required dropdowns (seniority + location)
- Most visitors likely leave without seeing a single number
- No clear CTA below the fold

**2. /qualify has a self-selection problem**
- Three buttons with zero explanation of what each path means
- No description of what happens after you click
- Ambitious fractionals self-select as in-seat → inflates application volume, deflates ICP rate
- ICP rate is only 13.9% — 86% of applicants are not the right fit

**3. 58% drop from viewing → starting the application**
- /join form opens cold — no social proof, no reinforcement of why it's worth 5 min
- Completely stripped of the member stories and logos visible on homepage
- "Become a Pavilion member in 5 minutes or less" is the only reassurance

**4. Homepage has 4 different CTA labels**
- "Join the room" / "Join B2B Tech Operators" / "Stop going solo" / "Join Now"
- All go to the same destination — dilutes intent and clarity

**5. No urgency or social proof at the moment of decision**
- /qualify and /join pages are bare
- Trust signals (Adobe, Salesforce, Microsoft logos, member quotes) only live on homepage
