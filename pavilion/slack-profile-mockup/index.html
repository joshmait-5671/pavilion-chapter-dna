🔨 I'm working on... New Field

Free text field. Members share what they're currently focused on — sparks conversations and surfaces collaboration opportunities.

Slack field type: Text (user editable)

💭 I'm wondering if... New Field

Free text field. A provocation — members share a hypothesis or hot take. Designed to spark debate and make profiles feel alive.

Slack field type: Text (user editable)

🍹 I need a drink New Field

Options list — members pick from a preset menu. Fun, low-stakes, instantly relatable. Changes with mood. Great icebreaker.

Slack field type: Options list (user selectable)

🏷️ Smart Tags Atlas Feature

Searchable, filterable expertise tags. Click "AI in GTM" → find every member with that tag. Powerful for connecting members who share focus areas.

Requires Slack Atlas · Smart Tag field type

📝 Extended Bio Available Now

Flexible Text supports up to 5,000 characters with rich formatting — bullet points, links, bold text, emoji. Much richer than the old one-liner.

Slack field type: Flexible Text (5,000 chars)

🔊 Name Pronunciation Available Now

Audio clip + phonetic text. Small touch, big inclusion signal. Shows members you care about getting the basics right.

Built-in Slack feature · Audio clip + text

🦁 Pavilion Crew New Field

Inspired by the "Hogwarts House" pattern from Slack's own docs. Members self-select into a crew/tribe — builds identity and belonging.

Slack field type: Options list (user selectable)

🏆 Recognition New Field

Badges and milestones visible on profile. Could be automated via Slack workflows or managed manually. Rewards the behaviors you want to see more of.

Slack field type: Text (admin managed) or integrated via app
