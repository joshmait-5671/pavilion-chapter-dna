## The Evolving Framework (Updated 2026-04-05)

DMF's job is fundamentally: **Who it's for. What it solves. How you build it.**

Every opportunity has three legs — and you can enter from any one:

- **WHO** — demographic, psychographic, or behavioral. Sometimes the sharpest insight is behavioral ("people who already paid for X once").
- **WHAT** — the value point. Not the product description — what does the person actually GET: relief, status, time, money, identity. This determines price, channel, and brand.
- **HOW** — product form, channel, model, stack. Sometimes you find a business model and work backwards to the audience.

**The magic is in the mashups.** Always ask: what works somewhere else that could work here? The intersection nobody else is looking at is where Josh wins.

No orthodoxy. This framework is a lens, not a checklist. Evolve it as Josh's reactions and real results teach us what actually works.
